Pasta Recipe

Description

Tasty Tomato Penne Pasta made with healthy ingridients and love.

You only need a pan and it will take not that much time

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomatos
  • Olive Oil
  • Cheese
  • Basilicum

Steps

  1. Wash Tomatos
  2. Put little bit of olive oil in the pan
  3. boil your water
  4. Put the pasta inside the boiling water
  5. Cut the Tomatos
  6. Mix the tomatos with Basilicum in the pan
  7. put out the pasta and mix it with the Tomato sauce
